Performance Tuning
===================

Performance Recording
----------------------
Tableau provide a performance review by producing a detail breakdown of the timing by the use of a dashboard.

1. Go to ``Help`` > ``Settings Performance`` > ``Start Performance Recording``

.. figure:: images/perf1.png
    :width: 450px
    :align: center
    :alt: alternate text
    :figclass: align-center
  
2. Do something, change a parameter, do a quick filter, etc.
3. Go to the same place and ``Stop Performance Recording``
4. A new Tableau worksheet will pop-up with the timing of each execution internal function.
