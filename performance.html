Performance¶

Performance Recording¶

Tableau provide a performance review by producing a detail breakdown of the timing by the use of a dashboard.

  1. Go to Help > Settings Performance > Start Performance Recording
  1. Do something, change a parameter, do a quick filter, etc.
  2. Go to the same place and Stop Performance Recording
  3. A new Tableau worksheet will pop-up with the timing of each execution internal function.
