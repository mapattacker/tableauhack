Field Calculations¶

Level of Detail (LOD)¶

LOD expressions is an important Tableau field calculation that allows calculations to subsets of one or more dimensions. There are 3 types of LODs: Fixed, Include, & Exclude. More in this link.

Below is an example. Red is the desired output. It should give a “Y” for types of Species, e.g., Cat, when just one or more contains a Disease marked by “0”. Original question from here.

#-- This will give 1 if any species have at least a disease
{FIXED [Species]:MAX([Disease])}

#-- Change the 1 to 'Y' and 'N' for otherwise
IF {FIXED [Species]:MAX([Disease])} = 1 THEN 'Y' ELSE 'N' END
